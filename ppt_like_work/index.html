⚠️ Problem Statement

Why Can't We Understand Cats?

Cats are the world's most popular pets — yet their emotions remain widely misunderstood.

🗣️

Limited Communication

Cats can't verbally express distress, pain, or happiness. Owners often misread behavioral signals entirely.

😰

Delayed Veterinary Care

Unrecognized negative emotions like fear or anger can delay necessary medical intervention and worsen health outcomes.

📉

No Automated Tools

No accessible, AI-powered system exists to decode cat emotions from everyday photos and audio clips.

💔

Weak Human–Cat Bond

Failure to understand emotional states results in reduced quality of interaction and weaker human–cat relationships.

✅ Our Solution — MewConnect
